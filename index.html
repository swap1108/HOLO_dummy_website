Hang out in Barcelona! NEW

Until a few days before we left and I didn’t reveal our location until we reached the security gate at the airport. I was so pleased with myself for managing to keep it a secret! I wanted the trip to be extra special . Barcelona is a city that goes 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and could give NYC a run for the “city that never sleeps” title. Be prepared for late-night dinners and parties til dawn. Barcelona knows how to get down.
